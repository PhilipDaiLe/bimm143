> structure? What does resid correspond to and how would you get a
> listing of all reside values in this structure?

The two non-protein resd values are: HOH (127) and MK1 (1)

The residue corresponds to the water being a stabilizing factor for the
protein and MK1 would be the drug ligand in 1hsg.
